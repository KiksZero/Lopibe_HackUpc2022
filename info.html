LoPibe The Game

Welcome to the LoPibe Game

In the LoPibe Game you will have to write the hidden sentence correctly and press enter before the time runs out.

Your objective is to get the highest score possible.

But how do you get some points?

Your task is to fill all the words of the sentence as fast as you can.

The faster, the more points you get! You will have 2 minutes to complete each sentence.

But be careful! If you miss, 10 seconds will be taken from your time.

Hints

Are you a bit lost? Don't worry! We got you covered.

Every second one random letter from the sentence will pop up. All you have to do is try to memorize them and you will be capable of solving the challenge.

As well, for every try you make, every letter you get right will be shown in the line above.
